Balsamic Vinegar Onions
-----------------------

* Ingredients
    * Onion
    * 1 Tabelspoon Balsamic Vinegar
    * 1 Tabelspoon Sugar (dark muscovado)
* Method
    * Sweat onion for 15min
    * Add vinegar + sugar + cook until sticky (5min)
